Intaking the Oils

Three Main Types of Oil Intake

Diffusion

About Diffusion

Essential Oil Diffusion is the process of dispersing essential oils so that their aroma fills a room or an area with the natural fragrance. From the simple to the elaborate, many different methods exist for diffusing essential oils into a room.

Roll-On

About Roll-On

Hand crafted and made with our synergy blends and single oils, our roll-on line is top of its class. They are easy, safe, convenient and travel friendly. Keep them in your backpack, purse or pocket. No mess, no fuss with these pre-diluted, ready to use roll-ons. Makes a great gift for the essential oil newcomer all the way to the experienced oil lover.

Capsule

About Capsules

One of the best ways to take essential oils internally is with the aid of capsules. Using capsules allows one to not only bypass any unpleasant tastes, but it also enables the oils to descend to the right area of the body before dissolving to allow for greater absorption.
